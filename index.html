Lake Norman State Park


About Lake Norman

Lake Norman, nestled in the heart of North Carolina, is a sprawling freshwater reservoir renowned for its scenic beauty and recreational opportunities. Spanning over 50 square miles, this man-made lake offers visitors a tranquil escape with activities like boating, fishing, and lakeside dining. Surrounded by charming towns and lush greenery, Lake Norman invites you to unwind amidst its serene waters and picturesque landscapes.
